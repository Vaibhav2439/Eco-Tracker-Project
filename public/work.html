Your EcoTrack Journey

Follow these steps to reduce your environmental footprint and contribute to a greener future.

1. Sign Up & Get Started

Create your EcoTrack account or log in to begin your journey towards sustainability.

2. Log Your Daily Activities

Input your daily habits like transportation, energy use, and waste management to track your impact.

3. AI Analyzes Your Footprint

Our AI calculates your carbon footprint and identifies areas for improvement.

4. Get Personalized Recommendations

Receive tailored eco-tips and actions to reduce your environmental impact effectively.

5. Take On Gamified Challenges

Join weekly challenges, complete quiz, and earn points to climb the leaderboard.

6. Track Your Impact in Real-Time

Visualize your progress with stats on CO₂ saved, water conserved, and trees planted.

7. Fund Global Eco-Projects

Use your points to support real-world initiatives like tree planting and renewable energy.
